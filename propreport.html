Investment Conclusion

Strong Investment Performance

The Crayfish Inn Restaurant property has demonstrated exceptional investment performance over the past 8 years, appreciating from R 3.6 Million to an estimated current market value of R 6.42 Million.

This represents a total appreciation of 78.4% over 8 years, or an annualized return of approximately 7.5% per annum - significantly outperforming average South African property market returns.

The combination of prime beachfront location, established business operations, and limited availability of similar properties in Ramsgate positions this investment for continued strong performance in the future.
